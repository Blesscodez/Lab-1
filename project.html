Our Proposed Project

Our project is an Online Marketplace Platform designed to connect small vendors with customers in a safe and affordable way. The system will use an integrated wallet to ensure that transactions are secure and trustworthy.

Project Objectives

  • Build a secure and user-friendly marketplace
  • Support both vendors and buyers with transparency
  • Prevent scams using escrow payment (wallet)
